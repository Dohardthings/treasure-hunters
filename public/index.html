About the treasure

One of the things that makes us special here at Nashville Treasure Hunters is that you will be searching for real treasure! Real silver, real gold, real precious and semi-precious stones, and a host of other valuables are up for grabs to enterprising adventurers! Below are a few examples of the kinds treasure that are found by our players right here in Nashville.

Silver Bullion

Our silver bullion is sourced from only the most reputable providers, like the American Precious Metals Exchange. We use only .999 FINE silver bullion. Sometimes it doesn't quite hit you until it's in your hands and you're holding it. This is real. And if you find it, it's yours!

Gold Bullion

Like our silver, the gold we use in our treasures is sourced only from reputable sources, and is incredibly pure, typically .9999 FINE gold. Can you imagine solving some riddles and finding gold hidden in a secret location? Because it's real. You can really do it.

Loose Gems and Jewelry

We get our gems and jewelry from all over the world, and put them in our treasures for that extra giggle factor when you open it. Occationally, a treasure will be made completely of jewels or a single large one (Those are small and sooo easy to hide well!) so you'll have to be keen eyed to find those!

How this works

“ ”

First, you review the treasure up for grabs and decide if you want to try and find it, and if the start date works for your schedule.

Next, buy into the treasure hunt using the paypal button above. This reserves a set of clues for you, as only a certain number of clue sets are released for each Treasure Hunt.

Then, on the release day (when the timer reaches "0") everyone who has bought in recieves an email with the clue/clues in it at the same time if there . Whom ever solves the clues fastest, and thus finds the treasure first.....keeps it. And gets HUGE bragging rights.

And last, but definitely not least, you celebrate! Bask in the glory of what you've accomplished! You are an adventurer, a detective, and a competetor with few equals! Display your winnings at home, make them into jewelry (if it isn't jewelry already), stash it as a rainy day investment, or just exchange it for cash! It's treasure.....you have options;)
